Security Overview

Our security policies are designed to protect both our systems and your data from unauthorized access and potential threats.

Important: All users must follow these security guidelines to maintain system integrity.

Password Requirements

All user accounts must adhere to the following password policies:

  • Minimum of 12 characters
  • At least one uppercase letter
  • At least one lowercase letter
  • At least one number
  • At least one special character
  • Changed every 90 days
  • No reuse of previous 5 passwords

Data Protection

We employ multiple layers of protection for your data:

  • End-to-end encryption for sensitive data
  • Regular security audits
  • Multi-factor authentication for all administrative access
  • Network intrusion detection systems

About File Tracking

Revolutionizing document management for modern organizations.

Our Mission

File Tracking was created to solve the common challenges organizations face with document management. Our mission is to provide a simple yet powerful solution for tracking, managing, and forwarding files across departments.

Traditional document management systems are often too complex or lack the specific features needed for efficient file tracking. File Tracking fills this gap with its intuitive interface and focused feature set.

Key Features

File Tracking

Know exactly where your files are at all times with our comprehensive tracking system. See who has accessed each file and when.

Workflow Automation

Streamline your document approval processes with automated workflows and notifications.

Role-Based Access

Control who can view, edit, and forward files with our granular permission system.

Audit Trail

Maintain a complete history of all file activities for compliance and reporting.
